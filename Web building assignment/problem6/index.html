Chemistry Assigment

Chemistory is the branch of science that deals with the properties, composition, and behaviour of matter. It is a fascinating field that explores the building blocks of the universe

Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a tabular arrangement of chemical elemenrs. Each element is represented by its chemical symbol. sucj as Fe for iron. H for hydrogen, and O for oxygen. Elements are classified in to groups and periods based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reaction are represented using chemical equations.Here's an example of a chemical reaction:

2H2 + O2 = 2H2O

This equation represents the reaction between hydrogen gas(H2) and oxygen gas (O2) to form water (H2).

Important Concepts

Stiochiometry is the calculation of reactants and products in chemical reaction. It involves balancing chemical equations to ensure mass and charges are conserved.

Subscripts indicate the number of atoms of each elemnt in a molecule, while superscripts represent charges or the number of atoms in an ion.
